archive of my classical recordings

Growing up an immigrant in Saint Louis, Missouri, music was a source of infinite joy and connection. It transformed me from a mute, scared girl, to a strong, self-aware leader. I began playing violin in third grade, switching to viola in middle school, and have performed in large and small ensembles all over the world. My favorite childhood memories include leading the viola section at Interlochen's World Youth Symphony Orchestra, founding the Clayton High School Buskers Club, and making lifelong friends at the Webster Chamber Music Preparatory Program. Enjoy classical music as an encounter with the divine. It is an expression of absolute beauty, magic, and life.
